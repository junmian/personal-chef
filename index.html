About the Chef

Chef Andy is an experienced personal chef based in Toronto, Ontario. He has travelled to more than 10 countries — including The Bahamas, Jamaica, China, Thailand, England, and Italy — before returning to Toronto where he now travels locally to provide personal chef services. He draws inspiration from his diverse clientele to craft menu items that consist of an eclectic mix of poultry, seafood, meat, and vegetarian dishes.
